www.biluminous.com

Biluminous is an online platform that connects Papua New Guinean women artisans with global buyers.

Just in time for APEC Leaders Week 2018, visitors to PNG can use Biluminous to purchase high-quality, locally-made bilum bags directly from a smartphone or computer. These distinctive Papua New Guinean string bags are skillfully hand-woven from plant and bark fibers and decorated using natural dyes gathered from the forests around each woman’s home village. As well as an exquisite traditional handicraft, a bilum bag is a customary gift for honored guests in Papua New Guinea.

Biluminous was created for the 2018 APEC App Challenge as a way to help PNG women artisans and micro-entrepreneurs begin selling their products online. By using the platform, weavers benefit from greater brand recognition, better market intelligence and a significantly larger share of the profits earned from each sale.

Customers of Biluminous take home a beautiful and useful souvenir, each with its own story and significance. Check back in early November when our site goes live – APEC delegates will be able to pick their bilums up from designated APEC venues within 24 hours of purchasing. Stay tuned!
